A few words about me

✨Once upon a time, I was designing cakes and baking pastries that could make anyone forget their diet. Life was sweet — literally.But one day, I saw a woman sitting in a café, working on her laptop, and I thought: “I wish I could be like her.” From that moment, I decided to run after my dream. From that moment, it felt like every programmer in the world was chasing me, whispering: “Come on, woman, this is your world!” So I jumped in. One and a half years later, I’ve completed a full-stack development course and now I’m mixing creativity, design, and code to build user-friendly websites. My toolkit includes HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, and Node.js — and I’m always learning more. I bring the precision of a baker, the creativity of a designer, and the problem-solving mindset of a developer. Whether it’s crafting pixel-perfect UIs or building responsive, functional apps, I’m ready to turn ideas into something people can actually use — and enjoy.
✈ So be quick and hire me… or others will hire me!🤭
